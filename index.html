Programs

Round 1 (April 10- April 24):
    Application form opens for 4 weeks (early April − late April)
    Candidates are required to submit: Personal information, Resume
Round 2 (April 26- middle May):
    Candidates will join Mentorship Program and workshop, then they will apply with new resume and essay to join Project X Internship Placement round 
    Project X recruitment team will assess the application based on candidate’s potentials to join Project X and employer partners
Round 3: Internship placement round (middle May-31 May) 
    Employers directly contact suitable candidates and conduct interview
    By successfully securing an internship with an employer partner, candidates are admitted into Project X Fellowship Program
